Modernize authentication with digital credentials.

MicroStrategy makes it easy for organizations to deploy digital credentials to employees, customers, suppliers, and partners. These credentials seamlessly sync with existing physical access control systems (PACS), logical systems, and user directories to streamline enterprise access and authentication as well as improve user productivity.

Digital Badges

Plastic ID badges, passwords, and tokens are insecure, inconvenient, and unable to identify security risks. Authenticate identity using secure, mobile badges on a user's smartphone.

Learn more

Embeddable and Customizeable

Use a single product to deploy digital credentials with unique access privileges to every employee, vendor, and customer. Deploy a standalone mobile app, or embed functionality within an existing, consumer-facing application.

Logical Access

Empower employees with secure access to any SAML-enabled application, website, VPN, or workstation. By eliminating the need for passwords, organizations improve workflow while generating valuable data on employee behavior and resource utilization.

Physical Access

Plug directly into a variety of existing enterprise PACS to quickly and cost-effectively modernize facilities access. Leverage GPS, Bluetooth, and biometric features to identify users and grant access to physical systems based on assigned privileges.

Generate and gather real-time identity intelligence

Usher delivers real-time views of data on enterprise access, authentication, and resource usage, and use this identity intelligence can be used to boost workforce productivity, monitor security, and optimize resource usage.

Operational Visibility

Connect with a widely distributed workforce. Whether it is a regional sales force, remote employees, or a network of truck drivers spread across the country, managers can locate and communicate with employees in just a few clicks, fostering greater collaboration throughout the organization.

Resource Monitoring

Organizations have a wide range of resources, from hardware and IT infrastructure to vehicles and office space. Generate and analyze data to better understand how and when resources are being used.

Real-time telemetry

Identity intelligence data can be analyzed in real time through live data processing via a Kafka-based ETL process. By putting real-time information into the hands of operations analysts, organizations can get detailed insight into user behavior, identify potential security threats, and make more data-driven business decisions.
