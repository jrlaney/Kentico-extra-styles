Accordion

A list of text items, which have expandable content blocks underneith when clicked.

Component features and rules:

  • Accordions will fill their parent components horizontally
  • Available in block or plain formats, with a number of toggle style and position options.
  • Used most often for FAQs and long technical content that lacks marketing value (technical information, asides, definitions).
