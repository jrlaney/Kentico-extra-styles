World-class enterprise analytics and mobility

MicroStrategy is committed to delivering world-class solutions for enterprise analytics, mobility, cloud, embedded analytics, and IoT. Our platform is built with an open framework that delivers support for multiple platforms and devices, as well as hundreds of optimized connectors to relational databases. With sophisticated administration and data connectivity tools, a unified metadata layer, and an unparalleled in-memory architecture, MicroStrategy offers everything you need in an analytics platform: enterprise-grade security, high performance, scalability, and data governance.

Comprehensive Analytics

MicroStrategy combines enterprise-grade performance, scalability, and security with easy-to-use interfaces and workflows, providing organizations with a single platform for analyzing all their enterprise data. From reporting and data discovery to mobile productivity and real-time telemetry, MicroStrategy offers the full breadth of capabilities required to transform data into real-world intelligence.

Integrate, Embed, and White-Label

Build custom applications and enhance your offerings with analytics by embedding MicroStrategy functionality into your products. White-label and rebrand web and mobile applications with the MicroStrategy SDK.

Open framework

Leverage a service-oriented architecture that is designed to enhance flexibility and extensibility and meet the demands of complex enterprise deployments. MicroStrategy’s open architecture and APIs, make it especially suitable for embedding BI content into enterprise portals, pushing external data into MicroStrategy cubes, extracting subsets of pre-processed data to be used in custom applications, creating custom data connectors, and integrating with third-party custom visualizations.

Object Oriented Architecture

MicroStrategy is built on a centralized metadata architecture that promotes fast development times, increased platform scalability, and efficient administration. MicroStrategy’s reusable and object oriented design allows every report, dashboard, and application to be reused hundreds of times by users across the enterprise, without any duplication of development effort.

Low Total Cost of Ownership

Benefit from centralized administration, and powerful tools that let you monitor, automate, and control your deployments with fewer administrators. Administrators can automate repetitive workflows, leverage tools for object management and migrations, regression testing, and monitor their environments with out-of-the-box reports and dashboards.

Flexible Multi-tenant Deployments

MicroStrategy supports a spectrum of multi-tenant architectures from high isolation to high sharing. In addition, MicroStrategy allows for personalization in a shared project, such as custom branding and logos for each tenant, personalized object names, and internationalization capabilities.

Support for Multiple Platforms and Devices

Support analytics on either Linux or Windows environments via on-premises, cloud, or hybrid deployment options. By partnering with AWS, the industry’s leading infrastructure as a service (IaaS) provider, MicroStrategy gives organizations around the world the ability to spin up instances in minutes, allowing companies to start small and expand as needed. Users can also consume and interact with data on iOS and Android devices.

High Performance and Scalability

The MicroStrategy platform was built with performance and scalability in mind. It is designed to deliver sub-second response times, even on large datasets, and support the analytical needs of hundreds of thousands of users across the enterprise.

Comprehensive Security

Implement single sign-on (SSO) while leveraging existing security infrastructure to provide a seamless experience for end users and rich functionality for IT administrators. Identity management systems, portals, and other business applications can all be integrated with MicroStrategy Web and Mobile for seamless access to enterprise data assets.
